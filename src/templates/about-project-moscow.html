Moscow – a place of beautiful opportunities

When people come to Moscow, many of them spot the rhythm and pace of the city. And it’s true that Moscow is a dynamically developing city today, where life goes non-stop. This opens new horizons in coming up with interesting business ideas and provides opportunities for business growth.

Moscow is a unique city for Russia in many ways. It is the only big city in the country that tends to become a financial center of international importance in the nearest future just like New York, London or Shanghai.

Moscow for travelling

Timeless arhitecture and historical places

Moscow fascinates not only with its pace of life, growth prospects, but also with the beauty of its architecture. Every year, Moscow occupies one of the leading places in the list of capitals that annually attract a maximum number of tourists. Most people from other countries associate Russia with the most famous Moscow landmarks such as: the Kremlin, Red Square, the Cathedral of Christ the Savior, Moscow State University. These are the most visited places year around.

Street views and original places

There are many cozy and old streets in the city center, as well as convenient and accessible pedestrian areas, walking through which brings a lot of positive emotions. The city has got modern parks and gardens, where you can escape from the city noise and entertain just the way you like. In winter, you can go to one of the biggest outside skating rinks: in the Gorky Park or VDNH (Exhibition of Achievements of National Economy).

There are a lot of museums and galleries in Moscow. Among them you can see both the world-famous giants of art and historical expositions (the Tretyakov Gallery, the Historical Museum, the Armories Chamber, the Pushkin Museum of Fine Arts) and small cultural institutions that are also interesting to visit.

Shopping, food culture and fun places

Nowadays shopping in Moscow is no worse than shopping in any large cities of Europe. There are many modern shopping centers, boutiques, where you can buy goods for every taste and budget: shoes, clothes, jewelry, accessories, junk jewelry, home appliances, etc.

The most popular department store in Moscow is GUM. This is a real architectural monument, which stretches along the Kremlin wall and attracts all tourists.

Not far from GUM, on Petrovka Street, there is another place shopping center called Petrovsky Passage. The Petrovsky Passage is located in a picturesque 19th century architecture building and is a monument of Russian architecture. A wide range of additional services are provided for customers; VIP service department, a bank office, ATMs, free ground parking, a hairdresser’s, a fast food area, a cozy coffee shop, a restaurant.

Moscow for business

Moscow becomes more and more attractive for business every year, providing large opportunities for companies: tax breaks for entrepreneurs, profitable offers for investors, convenient platforms for tenders.

Like any big city, Moscow constantly in demand for new enterprises in different fields of activity.

Business and investments

One of the important indicators of the developing market country is investment. The statistics of profitable investment areas allows you to control the feasibility of investing, analyze the impact on the economy, and make the right decisions. Attracting an investor to a business is a starting point for the development of an entrepreneurial project, whether it is an investment in a startup or an investment in a ready-made business with a long history. Once an investor is found, an entrepreneur receives the cherished funds for starting a business, and the investor has a chance of making a profit on relatively low risks. Such investments can bring a good income in the future, the main thing is to know where to invest and assess the risks in a correct way.

Real estate business

The most common and attractive way to save and "grow" your savings is to invest into real estate. Two main arguments speak in favor of real estate investments: first, investing in real estate is the most reliable type of savings because real estate prices are more stable and don’t collapse overnight in any unfavorable situation, the investor will have time to sell his property at an affordable price.

Secondly, the income from renting is quite predictable, and thanks to inflation, the cost of buildings is growing, even despite the small drawdowns during the crisis that always win back.

IT and technologies

Information technologies are one of the most rapidly developing branches of human activity; thus, many investors show special interest in this area. Information technologies have great potential and excellent prospects, and one should not forget that they also have specifics and high risks.

According to statistics, only 10–15% of projects reach the level of self-sufficiency and in the future begin to make a profit, but if it is successfully, the amount of profit can be just huge, which other areas of investment can’t even dream about.

An innovative idea and unique team competencies are main points for a successful project.

Other business potentials

It may seem that there are no areas that do not remain busy in a big city, but this approach is fundamentally wrong, but it’s rather vice versa.

Such city as Moscow constantly requires new enterprises in different fields of activity. Moscow is a perfect place to search for business contacts, there are different events for entrepreneurs and investors held every week, where you can bring your project or find an interesting startup to invest in.

The ideology of our foundation is an approximate distribution of funds in the ratio of 80% to fundamental projects (real estate), and 20% is invested in venture capital projects.

Some projects are invested, some we develop independently. Moreover, all our projects have between a synergy, real estate objects are also used by our IT companies for meetings, work, team building and recreation.
